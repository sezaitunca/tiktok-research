1. Purpose and Scope

This application is developed exclusively for academic research purposes as part of a TÜBİTAK-funded research project titled "Analysis of Misinformation and Manipulation Content on Social Media Platforms Using Natural Language Processing Methods."

The application is used solely for data collection and analysis to:

  • Study patterns of misinformation and manipulative content
  • Develop public policy recommendations
  • Advance academic understanding of digital information ecosystems

2. Academic Use Only

This application is NOT a commercial product or public service. It is a research tool used by authorized researchers at Alanya University under the supervision of the principal investigator.

3. Data Collection

3.1 What Data We Collect

We collect only publicly available content from social media platforms, including:

  • Public video metadata (likes, comments, shares, view counts)
  • Video captions and descriptions
  • Hashtags
  • Publication dates and timestamps
  • Publicly available user engagement metrics

3.2 What We DO NOT Collect

  • Private or restricted content
  • Personal identifying information beyond what is publicly available
  • Direct messages or private communications
  • Location data beyond what is publicly shared
  • Financial information

4. Data Usage

All collected data will be:

  • Used exclusively for academic research purposes
  • Anonymized and aggregated for analysis
  • Stored securely in compliance with academic data protection standards
  • Used only by authorized research team members
  • Published only in aggregated, non-identifiable forms in academic publications

5. Data Retention and Security

  • Data will be retained for the duration of the research project (12 months)
  • Data will be stored on secure university servers
  • Access is restricted to authorized research personnel only
  • Data will be deleted or archived according to university research data policies after project completion

6. No Commercial Use

This research and all collected data will NOT be:

  • Sold or shared with third parties for commercial purposes
  • Used for advertising or marketing
  • Used to develop commercial products or services

7. Compliance

This research complies with:

  • TÜBİTAK research ethics guidelines
  • Alanya University research ethics policies
  • Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK)
  • Academic research standards and best practices

8. User Rights

As this application only collects publicly available data for research purposes, individual users who have made their content public on social media platforms should refer to the terms of service of those platforms regarding their content.

9. Publications and Results

Research findings may be published in:

  • Academic journals and conferences
  • Research reports for public institutions
  • Educational materials for public awareness

All publications will present data in aggregated, anonymized form that does not identify individual users.

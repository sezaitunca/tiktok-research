Privacy Policy

Social Media Misinformation Research Application
Last Updated: February 15, 2026
Principal Investigator: Dr. Sezai TUNCA
Institution: Alanya University

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, processed, stored, and protected within the scope of the academic research project titled:

“Analysis of Misinformation and Manipulation Content on Social Media Platforms Using Natural Language Processing Methods.”

This application is developed exclusively for academic research purposes under a TÜBİTAK-funded project and is not a commercial service.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

Data processing is conducted on the basis of:

  • Academic research purposes serving public interest
  • Publicly available information
  • Compliance with Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK)
  • Compliance with applicable international data protection standards (including GDPR principles where applicable)
  • Institutional research ethics approvals

3. Categories of Data Processed

3.1 Publicly Available Data

The application processes only publicly accessible content obtained through authorized platform access mechanisms, including:

  • Public video metadata (view count, likes, shares, comments)
  • Video captions and textual descriptions
  • Hashtags
  • Publication timestamps
  • Public engagement metrics
  • Publicly visible usernames (for research indexing purposes only)

3.2 Data Not Collected

  • Private accounts or restricted content
  • Direct messages or private communications
  • Sensitive personal data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Geolocation data beyond what users publicly disclose

4. Purpose of Data Processing

Collected data is processed exclusively to:

  • Identify misinformation patterns
  • Analyze discourse structures
  • Conduct natural language processing (NLP) analyses
  • Generate aggregated research findings
  • Develop evidence-based academic insights

Data is not used for profiling, automated decision-making affecting individuals, or commercial analytics.

5. Data Anonymization and Aggregation

  • Usernames are pseudonymized or hashed before analysis
  • Data is aggregated at statistical levels
  • No identifiable individual-level data is disclosed in publications

Research outputs present patterns, trends, and analytical results without identifying specific users.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Stored on secure institutional servers
  • Accessible only to authorized research team members
  • Protected by institutional cybersecurity measures
  • Processed in compliance with academic data governance standards

7. Data Retention

Research data will be retained for the duration of the project (approximately 12 months) and archived or deleted according to Alanya University research data management policies upon project completion.

8. Data Sharing

Data will NOT be:

  • Sold to third parties
  • Shared for commercial purposes
  • Used for advertising
  • Provided to private entities for non-research activities

Aggregated datasets or methodological materials may be shared in anonymized form for academic transparency and reproducibility.

9. International Data Considerations

If data originates from users outside Turkey, processing is limited to publicly available information and conducted under internationally recognized academic research standards.

10. Individual Rights

  • Users retain rights over their content under the respective platform’s policies
  • No direct interaction with individual users occurs
  • Requests related to research data may be submitted to the Principal Investigator

11. Updates to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in research protocols or regulatory requirements. The “Last Updated” date indicates the most recent revision.
